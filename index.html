EDU AVE: Education For All

Mission Statement

Educational equity is a right every child deserves. Our mission is to fulfill this right and unlock students' full learning potential by providing the necessary resources for classroom success.

How can you help?

Simply fill out the Google form, where you'll list the school supplies you're donating, along with your address and contact information. We'll reach out to coordinate a convenient pick-up time — no extra effort needed from you!
